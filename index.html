This homework focuses on community engagement, immigrant integration, and reported speech with time and tense shifts.

1. Reading

Building Community Connections

Community engagement can help immigrants practice English, make new friends, and learn more about American culture. Volunteering, community centers, neighborhood associations, and online groups are all possible entry points.

These forms of participation do more than provide information. They help people build confidence, gain local knowledge, and create a sense of belonging over time.

When people later talk about these experiences, they often use reported speech. They may say that someone mentioned a conversation group, explained that a center offered classes, or said that volunteering had helped them feel more connected.
